Why Tech?

I come from Muzaffarpur, Bihar, and completed my education under the Bihar Board. During school, my knowledge of computers and technology was almost non-existent. But deep inside, I always had a strong curiosity — a desire to understand how technology works and how it shapes the world.

After 10th, I got my first mobile phone. That device became my gateway to the world of tech. I started exploring, learning, and understanding how digital systems function. Motivated by this growing interest, I chose Computer Science during my Diploma. That’s when my journey in tech truly began.

During my Diploma, I learned the basics of programming with C, Python, and Java, and also built a foundation in front-end web development. It wasn’t easy at first — it took time to understand concepts — but I never gave up. I kept learning at my own pace, one step at a time.

After joining B.Tech, my interest in Java grew stronger. I started building real projects like web applications, applying everything I had learned. Eventually, my curiosity led me to Android development, where I picked up Kotlin and began learning how to build mobile apps that actually solve real problems.

Today, I continue to expand my skills in both backend and mobile development. For me, technology is not just a field — it's a passion, a purpose, and a way to create impact. I'm building my future with code, one project at a time.
